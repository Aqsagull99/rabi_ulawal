A Brief History of Our Beloved Prophet Muhammad (SAWW)

The life of Prophet Muhammad (SAWW) serves as a model for humanity. His teachings and actions shaped the moral and spiritual foundation of Islam.

Important Events in Seerat-un-Nabi (SAWW)

  • Birth: Born in 570 CE in Makkah, in the tribe of Quraysh.
  • First Revelation: Received first divine revelation in Cave Hira in 610 CE.
  • Migration to Madinah: Hijrah to Madinah in 622 CE, marking the beginning of the Islamic calendar.
  • Treaty of Hudaybiyyah: Signed a peace treaty with Quraysh in 628 CE, a crucial turning point for Muslims.
  • Conquest of Makkah: Conquered Makkah in 630 CE, establishing peace without bloodshed.
  • Farewell Sermon: Delivered his final sermon during Hajj in 632 CE, emphasizing justice and equality.

Khatam-e-Nabuwat

Prophet Muhammad (SAWW) is the final prophet of Allah, sealing the line of prophethood. The belief in Khatam-e-Nabuwat (Finality of Prophethood) is a cornerstone of Islamic faith, affirming that no prophet will come after him.
